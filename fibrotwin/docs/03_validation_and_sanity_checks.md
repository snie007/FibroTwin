# 03 — Validation and Sanity Checks

1. **Patch test:** Affine displacement field under homogeneous material yields near-uniform strain.
2. **Uniaxial stretch sanity:** Right-edge displacement increases mean \(\varepsilon_{xx}\).
3. **Deposition kernel:** Single fixed cell gives radially decaying collagen map.
4. **Fibre normalization:** \(\|a\|=1\) after each update.
5. **Nonnegativity:** \(c\ge 0\).
6. **Run stability:** no NaN in displacement, collagen, fibres over 200 steps.
